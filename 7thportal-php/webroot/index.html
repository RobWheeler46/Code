Data and privacy

7thPortal stores the minimum information needed to operate, and keeps OSM as the source of truth for child, parent, programme, event, badge and payment data. Read the privacy notice for details.

7thPortal is not affiliated with Online Scout Manager. It reads information from OSM on your behalf and links back to OSM for actions.
