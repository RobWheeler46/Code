Your 7th Swindon front door

7thPortal brings together the OSM information parents, carers and leaders use most - linked children, section details, programme, events and badges - in one clean, mobile-friendly place. OSM stays the source of truth: 7thPortal links back to it for updates, event sign-up and payments.

What 7thPortal is for

Parents and carers can see their child's section, upcoming programme and events, and badge progress at a glance. Leaders get a section dashboard to help prepare for meetings. Anything that needs updating - contact details, event responses, payments - is done in OSM, with a clear link back from every relevant page.

Data and privacy

7thPortal stores the minimum information needed to operate, and keeps OSM as the source of truth for child, parent, programme, event, badge and payment data. Read the privacy notice for details.

7thPortal is not affiliated with Online Scout Manager. It reads information from OSM on your behalf and links back to OSM for actions.
