Disconnect from OSM

What disconnecting does

  • Removes the encrypted access and refresh tokens held by this application.
  • Ends your application session.
  • Records the disconnection in the audit log.

What it does not do

Disconnecting here removes only the tokens held by this application. It may not remove any authorisation recorded within OSM itself. If you need to withdraw the application's access at the OSM end as well, review it in OSM.

No information in OSM is changed by disconnecting.
